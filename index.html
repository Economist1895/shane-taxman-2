HOW TO PLAY

GOAL

Defeat all enemies on the map. You lose if all your units fall.

TURN FLOW

Each round has a Player Phase then an Enemy Phase. On your phase, tap an ally to act. A unit that has acted is greyed out until next round.

ACTING WITH A UNIT

  1. Move — blue tiles show where you can go. Tap one to move there (or stay put).
  2. Act — attack a red tile, use an ability on a green tile, or tap Wait to end the turn.

COMBAT

When you pick a target, a Combat Forecast shows damage and hits for both sides. Defenders counter-attack if in range. Fighters with much higher SPD strike twice.

STATS

  • HP Health. At 0 the unit is defeated.
  • ATK Attack power. Raw damage before defense.
  • DEF Reduces physical damage (soldiers, archers).
  • RES Reduces magic damage (mages).
  • SPD If your SPD beats your opponent's by 4+, you strike twice.
  • RNG Attack range in tiles. Archers strike at 2, mages 1–2, soldiers 1.
  • MOV Tiles movable per turn (normally 2; blocked by trees/water).

CLASSES

  • Soldier — melee, deals physical damage, blocked by DEF.
  • Archer — ranged 2 tiles, physical damage, blocked by DEF.
  • Mage — range 1–2, magic damage, blocked by RES.

TERRAIN

Trees and water block movement — no one can step on them. Ranged attacks still fly over both.

ABILITIES

Each hero has a unique PASSIVE (always on) or ACTIVE (once per turn, in place of attacking) skill. Check the squad-select screen for full descriptions.

TIPS

  • Lead with tanks (high DEF/HP). Keep squishy DPS and healers behind.
  • Use terrain to funnel enemies — they can't path through trees or water.
  • Check the forecast before every attack — a bad trade can snowball.
